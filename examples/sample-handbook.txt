WESTBROOK HIGH SCHOOL
STUDENT HANDBOOK — SAMPLE

Welcome to Westbrook High School. This handbook is intended to help students, parents, and staff understand school policies, procedures, and expectations.

ACADEMIC POLICIES
----------------
Grading Scale:
A  90 - 100
B  80 -  89
C  70 -  79
D  60 -  69
F  0 -   59

Attendance:
Students must attend at least 90% of class meetings to receive credit. More than 10 unexcused absences in a semester may result in loss of credit.

Homework Policy:
Homework is assigned regularly to reinforce classroom learning. Late work may receive a reduced grade unless the student has an approved excuse.

STUDENT CODE OF CONDUCT
-----------------------
Respect:
Students are expected to treat peers, teachers, and staff with courtesy and respect.

Dress Code:
Clothing should be clean, appropriate for school, and not contain offensive language or images.

Technology Use:
School devices and internet access are provided for educational purposes only. Unauthorized use may result in restricted access.
